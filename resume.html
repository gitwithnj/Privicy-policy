Kquest Academy (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Kquest Academy mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and what choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways when you use the App:

A. Information you provide directly

  • Admission enquiry forms: student name, parent/guardian name, phone number, class of interest, and optional message text.
  • Announcements (authorized staff only): title, message body, category tag, and optional file attachments (for example PDFs, images, or documents up to 10 MB).
  • AI doubt-solver (Kquest Tutor): questions and messages you type in the chat, along with a session identifier used to show your conversation history during that session.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and app usage data: basic technical information needed to run the App, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and network connectivity.
  • Session identifiers: a randomly generated session ID for the AI chat feature so your messages in that session can be retrieved.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs: if enabled by your device or app platform, limited error logs to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

C. Information we do not collect

  • We do not require account registration or login for general use of the App.
  • We do not collect precise GPS location through the App.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display academy announcements, programs, and contact information.
  • Process and respond to admission enquiries submitted through the App.
  • Provide the Kquest Tutor AI doubt-solving feature and maintain chat history for a session.
  • Store and deliver announcement attachments you choose to upload (staff use).
  • Improve App performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. AI Processing (Kquest Tutor)

When you use the AI doubt-solver, your messages are sent to our backend servers and processed using Google Gemini (via Emergent’s LLM integration) to generate educational responses. Chat messages may be stored in our database linked to a session identifier so you can view recent conversation history in the App.

Do not enter sensitive personal information (such as full home address, government ID numbers, financial details, or health records) into the chat unless necessary for your academic question.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: hosting, database (MongoDB), and AI processing providers that help us operate the App, under contractual obligations to protect your data.
  • App stores: Google Play and/or Apple App Store, which may collect data according to their own policies when you download or update the App.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of Kquest Academy, our users, or others.

We do not share your information with third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on secure servers operated by our hosting and database providers. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) for data transmitted between the App and our servers.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Enquiry submissions: retained as long as needed to process admissions and maintain business records.
  • Announcements: retained while published and for reasonable archival periods thereafter.
  • Chat messages: retained to support session history and service improvement; we may delete older chat data periodically.
  • Uploaded files: retained while associated with an announcement unless deleted by authorized staff.

7. Children and Students

The App is intended for students and parents associated with Kquest Academy. If you are under 18, please use the App with the knowledge and consent of a parent or guardian. Parents may contact us to review or request deletion of information submitted on behalf of a minor.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of information you submitted (subject to legal or business record requirements).
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Permissions Used by the App

The App may request device permissions for specific features:

  • Internet / network access: to load announcements, programs, chat, and enquiry submissions.
  • Document / file access: to attach optional files when creating announcements (authorized use).
  • Phone / dialer (via system link): when you tap a phone number to call Kquest Academy.
  • Maps (via system link): when you choose to open our location in a maps application.

You can manage many permissions in your device settings.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may open external services (such as phone dialer, maps, or file download links). Those services are governed by their own privacy policies, not this one.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located outside your state or country, including by our cloud and AI service providers. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place when data is transferred.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
